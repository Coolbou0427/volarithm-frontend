How It Works

The bot watches the Bitcoin price and trades based on our strategy and algorithm. We based this on simulated profit using historic numbers. We only bet a fraction of the total amount of money in your account so one bad day doesn't lose all your money at once.

  • Watches prices in real time
  • Uses our tested strategy to buy or sell based on multiple factors
  • Only trades using a small amount of your total money invested
  • Shows a history of trades so you can clearly see where your money is going

Trading Strategy

We focus on the daily UP/DOWN market for Bitcoin. The bot does not and can not predict the outcome of the bitcoin bet. Instead it aims to catch general trends and the direction of bitcoin for that day. It will also bet more or less based on the volatility of bitcoin as well as whether or not there is a clear trend up or down.

Risk Management

We use small daily bets so it doesn't bet too much in one day, and it tries to sell it's position to reduce losses in the case that might lose. You can also pause the bot from trading your money at any time from the Profile page.
